Bakery Vocabulary

Avoid an awkward Boulangerie experience and use this table to learn your croissants from your chausson aux pommes.

Photo Name Description Ingredients/Allergens
Croissant A flaky, buttery pastry known for its crescent shape and delightful layers. butter, flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and milk (Lactose, Gluten)
Pain au Chocolat A buttery pastry very similar to a croissant, but filled with chocolate. chocolate, butter, flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and milk (Lactose, Gluten)
Baguette de Tradition A more rustic baguette with a firm crust and rich flavor flour, salt, and yeast (Gluten)
Chausson aux Pommes The french version of an apple turnover, but instead, it's called an apple slipper! butter, applesauce, flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and milk (Lactose, Gluten)
